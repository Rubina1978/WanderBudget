How to ignite the wanderlust

Wanderlust lives in all of us. But too often, it gets buried under excuses: not enough money, not enough time, not the right moment.

All we have to do is clear away those doubts and let the universe show us what’s possible. Because adventure isn’t just for the wealthy, the fearless, or the lucky ones — it’s for anyone willing to take the first step.

Paul Coelho said in one of his books "If you really want something all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it".

So ask yourself, do you really want to travel or are you just day dreaming? What is stopping you? - money, time, travel buddy, partner, fear of failure, fear of missing home, family, friends, children, courage, health?

Here is the thing - travel itself is not difficult, but to take a leap, close the door of the known, familiar, comfortable and venture out of your comfort zone to the unknown is.
